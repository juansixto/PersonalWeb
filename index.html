Biography

Juan Sixto is a Research Intern at the MoreLab Research Group at DeustoTech - Deusto Institute of Technology. He finished his studies of Computer Engineering in 2012 at the University of Deusto. He is currently taking part in projects about Natural Lenguage Processing (NLP), opinion mining, machine learning and social network analysis.
